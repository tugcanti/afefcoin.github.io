About AFEF Token

AFEF is a community token built for strategy game lovers. Our mission is to ensure transparency, fair distribution, and long-term value for our holders. We plan to introduce governance voting, staking rewards, and game-linked features as the project evolves.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 4,950,000 AFEF

40% Liquidity Pool

A significant portion is locked in liquidity pools to ensure market stability and trust.

30% Airdrop & Community

Dedicated to rewarding our early adopters, supporters, and active community members.

20% Development & Marketing

Fuels continuous project growth, new features, and global marketing campaigns.

10% Team & Advisors

A vested allocation for the core team and advisors, locked to align with long-term goals.
