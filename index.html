What is AFEF?

AFEF is a community token designed for strategy game fans. Our mission is transparency, fair distribution, and long-term value creation. We plan airdrops for early adopters, community governance, and token-based rewards.

Benefits

Governance

Token holders will have the right to vote on roadmap and key decisions.

Rewards

Staking and special airdrops will provide advantages to early supporters.

Gaming Potential

Future integration ideas may offer exclusive in-game benefits for token holders.

Transparency

The contract is verifiable on Etherscan; airdrop and liquidity events will be publicly shared.
