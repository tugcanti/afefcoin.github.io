Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is AFEF Token?

AFEF is a community-driven fan token created for strategy game lovers. It aims to offer exclusive advantages and a say in the project to its holders.

How can I join the airdrop?

You can join the airdrop by filling out the form in the "Join the Airdrop" section above. You just need to enter your name, email, and ERC-20 wallet address.

Is the project secure?

Yes, security is our top priority. Our smart contracts will be audited by a reputable third-party firm before deployment. The results will be shared transparently with the community.
