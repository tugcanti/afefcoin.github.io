What is AFEF Coin?

AFEF Coin is a fan token specially designed for the community of the legendary strategy game, Age of Empires Mobile. It offers players and fans the opportunity to have a say in the project's development, participate in exclusive events, and potentially gain in-game advantages in the future.

Our mission is to empower our community by merging the Age of Empires universe with blockchain technology, adding real value to our token holders.
